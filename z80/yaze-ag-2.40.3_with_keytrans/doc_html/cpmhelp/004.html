DATE        



  Syntax:

       DATE {CONTINUOUS}
       DATE {time-specification}
       DATE SET

  Explanation:

  The DATE command lets you display and set the date  and  time  of
  day. The date and time may be in US, UK or Year-Month-Day format,
  depending on the current setting - see SETDEF.
