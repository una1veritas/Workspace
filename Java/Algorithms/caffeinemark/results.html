DISCLAIMER

Since the participants in this survey have a multitude of configurations and administered the test themselves, our survey is not a controlled scientific study. Though we have made an effort to accurately report submitted benchmarks, some errors may be present in our database. If you detect any errors or omissions, please report them to us.

IMPORTANT NOTICE:

CaffeineMark 3.0 scores cannot be compared on an equal footing with scores from earlier versions of the CaffeineMark.

CaffeineMark 3.0 consists of new tests and the overall CaffeineMark score is computed using a different scoring/averaging scheme.
